Manufacturing partnerships

With world-class manufacturing facilities operating at global business standards, Handok has helped partners successfully produce a variety of dosage forms for their products for Korea and for export to over 50 countries. In 2012, Handok manufactured over 130 products in almost 200 presentations. Tablet production in the same year exceeded 1.0 billion tablets across all formulations.

Handok's recognized quality policy

Handok consistently strives to meet the highest standards of quality. This has been validated by the fact that no deficiency has been identified in inspections by the Korean MFDS. Handok’s quality level has been qualified through numerous audits by global pharmaceutical companies, including GSK, AstraZeneca, Bayer, Sanofi, Abbott, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Novartis, Roche, Boehringer Ingelheim, and Merck and certified by several authorities including 3 PIC/S countries, Taiwan, Ukraine and South Africa.

Handok’s Quality Management Systems refer to global standards like EU GMP, PIC/S, cGMP, WHO, ICH guidelines and are managed as currently updated and implemented. We have much experience of working with the quality function of global pharmaceutical companies and achieving good Quality KPI results.

In addition, Handok has won numerous awards in the field of health, safety, and environment. We have been designated a "Green Company" in 2008 by the Ministry of Environment every year for over a decade and have been continually recognized by the President of Korea and various government ministries. In 2012, we achieved "Twofold zero accident record", awarded by the Korea Occupational Safety and Health Agency (KOSHA 18001), Occupational Health and Safety Advisory Services (OHSAS 18001) and International Organization for Standardization (ISO 14001) with continuous success of reacquisition every year.

As a consequence, many leading pharmaceutical companies have partnered with Handok to assure quality production of key products.
